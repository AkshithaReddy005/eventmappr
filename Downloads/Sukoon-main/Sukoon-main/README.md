# SoulSpace
Experience holistic wellness through our carefully crafted journeys for mind, body, and soul.

## 📃 Description
"Welcome to SoulSpace - your gateway to holistic wellness! Our platform offers unique wellness experiences designed to nourish your mind, body, and soul. From mood-enhancing music to mindful movement, creative expression to community connection, we provide a comprehensive ecosystem for your well-being journey. Whether you're seeking relaxation, inspiration, or personal growth, SoulSpace is here to support you every step of the way."

## 🌟 Our Features

### Our Unique Wellness Experiences
Curated journeys for your mind, body, and soul

→ **MoodTunes**
Elevate your mood with handpicked playlists and soundscapes.

→ **MindFuel Reads**
Inspiring books and articles to spark growth and positivity.

→ **ZenMoves**
Guided yoga and mindful movement sessions for all levels.

→ **JoyBurst Sessions**
Laughter therapy and joy-boosting activities.

→ **HeartTalks**
Supportive conversations and uplifting community stories.

→ **Little Joys**
Wholesome content and activities for children and families.

→ **Rhythm & Flow**
Dance and movement to unlock your creative energy.

→ **ColorSoul**
Art therapy and creative expression for relaxation.

→ **PlayMinds**
Mindful games and puzzles to stimulate and relax your mind.

## 💼 Our Services <a name = "services"></a>
- **Audio Therapy:**
  Browse through our collections of recommended peacful music, motivational podcasts, and inspirational audiobooks. 
- **Reading Therapy:**
  Browse through some inspirational quotes, book summaries, videos, and reccomended books to read when your are stressed out.
- **Yoga Therapy**
  Discover the benefits of yoga, read about popular yoga poses, and watch video tutoroials that you can follow along with.
- **Laughing Therapy**
- **Talking Therapy**
  Find out if you are someone who can benefit from talking therapy and go through our provided articles and videos about talking therapy to help manage depression, anxiety, and stress.
- **Consult A Doctor**
  Sukoon provides you with a list of experienced professionals that you can contact if you want to take the next step in handling your stress. 

## 🚀  Get Started <a name = "getStarted"></a>
Every contribution counts.
1. Ensure that you have Git installed and working properly.
2. Fork the repo by clicking on 'Fork' above.
3. Clone the project by running git clone <forked_project_url>.
4. Confused about where to start? Check out [good-first-issue](https://github.com/Susmita-Dey/Sukoon/labels/good%20first%20issue).
5. Make a separate branch with the issue name ex. issue#485.
6. You are good to go. Change the code and we will be waiting for your exciting PRs.

For contributing guidelines and standards, visit [contributing.md](https://github.com/Susmita-Dey/Sukoon/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
